How Crypta Turned a Manual Bottleneck Into a Seamless Client Experience

Manual onboarding was slowing growth and weakening the client experience.

As Crypta scaled its IT services across web development, SEO, and digital content, one internal bottleneck persisted: the manual client onboarding process.


Each new client triggered a chaotic series of admin tasks — filling out spreadsheets, chasing missing information, drafting project briefs, and sending email confirmations. These repetitive workflows were not just inefficient; they also introduced inconsistencies that made the company appear less professional than the quality of its work deserved.


To solve this, Crypta partnered with BlackBox to build an automated onboarding system that delivers speed, consistency, and professionalism at scale. By integrating structured data collection, centralized tracking, and instant documentation, the new workflow gives Crypta’s team a seamless way to welcome clients and launch projects — all in under a minute.

A tailored, automated workflow that unlocks scale and professionalism.

Working closely with Crypta, we designed a client onboarding system that was both powerful and easy to maintain. The goal wasn’t just to save time — it was to build a repeatable, scalable process that reflected the high standards of the company’s brand.


The core of the system was built using free and accessible tools: Google Forms, Sheets, Docs, and Gmail, all orchestrated through Make (formerly Integromat). With this setup, Crypta could:


• Collect detailed project briefs through a branded intake form

• Automatically route and clean client data into a master sheet

• Instantly generate a one-page Google Doc summary for each project

• Send confirmation emails to the client and notifications to the internal team — all within moments of submission


What once required hours of back-and-forth now happens in under a minute — with zero manual effort.

Bringing consistency, clarity, and control to the onboarding process.

The most powerful part of the transformation wasn’t just the automation itself. It was what that automation enabled.


Project briefs were now consistent across every client, giving internal teams immediate clarity on deliverables, timelines, and budgets. Client data lived in one source of truth, searchable and trackable. And first impressions — a critical part of client satisfaction — were now elevated through professional, timely responses.


What had been a patchwork of emails and copy-pasted notes became a structured, reliable workflow that required no technical expertise to maintain or update.

Crypta’s onboarding is now faster, smarter, and future-ready.

Crypta’s new onboarding system transformed more than a workflow. It elevated their operational maturity and allowed their team to focus on what matters most: delivering great client work.


By automating intake and documentation, Crypta reclaimed valuable hours each week and reduced the risk of human error. It was a small investment with large ripple effects — improving the client experience, team coordination, and business scalability.
